# Vocal Coach System

The Vocal Coach remains clip-focused, while Studio Intelligence adds project-level context.

## Real Measurements

- Average cents error
- Pitch drift
- Voiced percentage
- Phrase count
- Timing grid offset
- Vocal consistency
- Sibilance, nasal, thin, mud, and harshness approximations
- Clipping and quiet-take detection

## Actions

- Safe cleanup
- Autotune handoff
- Level riding
- Punch-in marker creation
- Double and harmony draft suggestions
- Creative FX suggestions when energy and spectral conditions support them

Creative suggestions are attached to evidence such as phrase energy, section role, harshness, and user dryness preference.
